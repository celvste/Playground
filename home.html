Welcome to PLAYGROUND:

Singapore's Toy Story

Step into a world of wonder and nostalgia at PLAYGROUND, where Singapore's rich toy heritage comes to life!

Our unique exhibition takes you on a thrilling journey through time, exploring the evolution of play in our vibrant city-scape. Reconnect with familiar toys from your childhood or learn more about the games your parents and their parents used to bond over. Take our hand for a chance to be a kid all over again!
